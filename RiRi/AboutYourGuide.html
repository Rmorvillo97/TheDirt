Maria "RiRi" Gigliello

Hiker by day, Blogger by night... Mixed Drinker year round

I have had a wide variety of experience in multiple jobs, internships and volunteer opportunities. As an environmental student, I have interned at The Wild Center as a Naturalist and currently hold a position on Siena's campus as a Sustainability Coordinator. As a writer I have also presented my research on women in the film industry at Siena College's First Year Seminar Research Conference and have been published in two on-campus literary magazines, Pendragon and Gleanings.


Maria Gigliello was named to the Siena College President's List for the Spring 2019 Semester. Gigliello is from Schenectady, NY.

The President's List requires a 3.9 grade point average or higher.
